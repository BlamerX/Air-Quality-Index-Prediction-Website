About Us

Weather AQI India is a website dedicated to providing real-time weather and Air Quality Index (AQI) information for various locations across India. We believe in enabling people to make informed decisions about their outdoor activities and health by staying aware of the current air quality and weather conditions.

Our platform pulls data from reliable sources and utilizes sophisticated algorithms to calculate and present the AQI. The AQI helps you understand the quality of air in your area, empowering you to take precautions and make choices for better health and well-being.

Whether you're planning your day, engaging in outdoor activities, or simply concerned about the air you breathe, Weather AQI India is here to provide you with accurate and up-to-date information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about air quality and its impact on public health. By making AQI data easily accessible, we aim to empower individuals, communities, and organizations to take proactive steps towards improving air quality and protecting their well-being.

We believe that by fostering a culture of environmental responsibility and accountability, we can make a positive impact on the air we breathe and contribute to a healthier and more sustainable future for India.
